Our Programs

From complete beginners to advanced racers, we have a program tailored to your skill level and goals.

Beginner Downhill

Learn the fundamentals of downhill mountain biking in a safe, controlled environment.

  • Bike setup & body position
  • Braking & cornering basics
  • Trail reading & line selection
  • Introduction to small drops

Intermediate Techniques

Take your skills to the next level with more technical terrain and advanced techniques.

  • Advanced cornering & berms
  • Drop techniques (1-3 feet)
  • Small jumps & tabletops
  • Speed control & pumping

Advanced Racing

For experienced riders looking to compete or push their limits on extreme terrain.

  • Large drops & gaps (3-6 feet)
  • High-speed technical sections
  • Race strategy & mental prep
  • Bike setup for racing
