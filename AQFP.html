Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend measurements be accurate to within 1/8 inch. Our design team can help verify your measurements if needed, or we can arrange for a professional measurement service for an additional fee.

Your flat pack includes all pre-cut cabinet components, doors, drawers, shelves, and all necessary hardware (hinges, handles, screws, etc.). We also include detailed assembly instructions with diagrams.

Our cabinets are designed for straightforward assembly with basic tools (drill, screwdriver, level). Most customers with moderate DIY skills can assemble them in a weekend. We also offer video tutorials and phone support if you get stuck.

We offer a range of materials including plywood (various grades), MDF, solid wood (oak, maple, walnut), and melamine. Each material has different price points and characteristics that we can discuss during your consultation.

Typical lead time is 3-5 weeks depending on project complexity and material availability. Simple projects can sometimes be completed in 2 weeks, while very large or complex projects may take up to 6 weeks.
